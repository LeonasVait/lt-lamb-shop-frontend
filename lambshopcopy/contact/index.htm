Frequently Asked Questions

Please read our FAQ before sending us a message.

What animals do you slaughter?

We slaughter Beef, Pork, chicken and Lamb.

How are your cows treated prior to slaughter?

Our beef is grain fed. Our beef is steroid free.All animals, if ever on a medication, are off all medications for six weeks prior to slaughter.Our beef is raised by farm families, who, in many cases, we have been dealing with for three generations. These farmers take very good care of their animals.

Do you sell grass fed beef?

Grass fed beef is available upon request. We sell grass fed beef by the side (half) only.Delivery may take longer as we only process a grass fed animal once both sides have been purchased

How are your pigs treated prior to slaughter?

Our pork is grain fed. All hogs, if ever on a medication, are off all medications for six weeks prior to slaughter. Again, our farmers take very good care of their animals.

Do you provide fresh lamb?

Fresh lamb is available upon request. Again, all lambs are medication free for six weeks before slaughter.

Fresh lamb is available upon request. Again, all lambs are medication free for six weeks before slaughter.

Why don’t you have prices on your website?

Pricing changes based on the weekly market price.
